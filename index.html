Subscription and Billing

Decidel Pro subscriptions are managed through Apple. To cancel or modify your subscription, go to Settings > Apple ID > Subscriptions on your iPhone.

Frequently Asked Questions

What AI providers are supported?

Decidel supports OpenAI, Anthropic, Google Gemini, and Groq. Bring your own API key from any provider to enable AI-powered summaries.

Is my data stored on your servers?

No. Decidel has no backend servers. Your API keys are stored locally in the iOS Keychain. Article content is sent directly from your device to your chosen AI provider.

How do I restore my Pro subscription?

Open Decidel, go to Settings > Decidel Pro, and tap "Restore Purchases". Make sure you're signed in with the same Apple ID used for the original purchase.
